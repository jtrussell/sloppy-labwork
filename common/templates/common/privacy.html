Privacy Policy

Last updated: Apr/21/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use sloppylabwork.com (the “Site”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you register, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and other information that links your account with an associated social account in cases where 3rd party SSO was used.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with the Site, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to provide essential functionality and improve user experience. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (for EU Users)

We collect and use your data based on one or more of the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (e.g., for non-essential cookies)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., when you create an account)
  • Legal obligation
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving services)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, personalize, and improve our services
  • To manage your account and respond to your inquiries
  • To monitor usage and security of the Site
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g. cloud hosting). Where data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) are in place.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above, or as required by law.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Global Privacy Laws)

If you are located in the EU, UK, or certain other jurisdictions, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing)
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format
  • To exercise your rights, please contact us at inquiries@sloppylabwork.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without parental consent.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: inquiries@sloppylabwork.com
