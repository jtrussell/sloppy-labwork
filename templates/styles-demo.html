Surface

.surface and its variants

.surface

Default — surface background (slightly raised feel against the page). Used by .unmatched-player-card.

.surface.surface--flat

Transparent background — inherits whatever's behind it. Used by .lineup-card, .version-card, .deck-card, .note-item, and .empty-state.

.surface.surface--sunken

Page background — sits "deeper" than a surface, useful for grouped lists. Used by .players-list.
