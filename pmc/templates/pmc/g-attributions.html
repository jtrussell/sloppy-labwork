Overview

KeyChain is an unofficial, community-made platform built to enhance and support organized play for KeyForge, the unique card game created by Richard Garfield and published by Ghost Galaxy. The use of the KeyForge intellectual property, illustrations, and other brand assets are used under Ghost Galaxy's Intellectual Property Use Policy. KeyForge and the KeyForge logo are registered trademarks of Ghost Galaxy, Inc. All Rights Reserved.
