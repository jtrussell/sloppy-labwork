About Keychain

Global:
Elements that are account-wide, across all playgroups.
Playgroup:
Elements that are within the context of an individual playgroup.

Level (Lv)

Your Level (Lv) is an at-a-glance view of your overall progression and participation in KeyForge organized play through KeyChain, earned over time by accruing Experience Points (XP). Your level appears on your player profile, and additional personalization items can be unlocked through leveling up. Higher levels generally require more XP to level up.

Experience Points (XP)

Experience Points (XP) are earned for general participation in KeyForge organized play across all playgroups, and serve as the foundation for your overall KeyChain account progression. Earning XP is how you level up. XP is awarded as such:
  • Event Attended: 25 XP
  • Match Won: 10 XP
  • Match Lost: 5 XP

Rank Points (RP)

Rank Points (RP) are a leaderboard ranking metric used for determining player placements and standings across events. There are two types of RP:
  1. Global RP: Calculated from events across all playgroups, using predefined RP value mappings.
  2. Playgroup RP: Calculated internally from events within a single playgroup, using RP value mappings set by the Playgroup EO.
RP can be filtered by Month, Year, or All-Time, allowing standings to be measured across different timeframes.
